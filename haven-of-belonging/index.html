We ease their pain.

We mitigate their sorrow.

We uplift their hearts.

When the heart is as large as Ohr Chodosh’s, every girl and boy feels right at home.

In the Ohr Chodosh home, the heartbeat of compassion guides us to attend to every detail of an orphan’s journey. From mentorship programs to engaging events, we create a nurturing haven where each child feels valued and supported. Our initiatives extend beyond immediate needs, reaching into the hearts of educators and the broader community.

Haven of Belonging

Shabbatons

Haven of Peace

The Ohr Chodosh Shabbatons are a beacon of hope for girls and
boys, serving as the annual highlight for every child. These
gatherings are uplifting and enjoyable, where everlasting
meaningful connections are forged. The participants leave on a
high, feeling recharged both physically and spiritually, empowered
to face any challenges that come their way upon returning home.

Professional Help

Haven of Help

A team of professionals is prepared to step in when needed, particularly for sensitive matters. They are also available to engage with mosdos and Daas Torah and facilitate communication when necessary.

Shiva Packages

Haven of Comfort

Whether anticipated or sudden, one can never be adequately prepared for the emotional challenges of a loss. This is where the Ohr Chodosh’s shivah package comes into play. Offered promptly upon experiencing a loss, it is meticulously stocked with comprehensive guidance on halachos, steadfast support and a collection of insightful articles pertaining to shivah. A first aid package for the soul.

Publications

Haven of Insight

Incredibly relatable! Dive into a 100-page magazine overflowing with personal anecdotes, uplifting topics, relatable Q&As, insightful articles, and more. This delightful publication graces mailboxes several times a year, bringing inspiration and connection.

Training

Haven of Safety

No matter how well-trained an educator may be, handling the needs of orphans in the unfortunate event of a tragedy is a unique challenge. Whether it occurs within the school setting or at home, the well-being of the children are equally important. As a result, workshops are provided for educators to ensure they are equipped with the necessary tools to address these specific challenges.

Hotline

Haven of Connection

A lifeline for numerous children reaching out daily, the hotline offers a wealth of support. From topics of encouragement, relatable Q&As, and insightful articles to the option of learning Mishnayos, it is a source of inspiration for callers seeking guidance and motivation. 

Melava Malka & Chevras Tehillim

Haven of Togetherness

Whether it’s winter or summer, long Shabbos afternoons and Motzei Shabbosim are both well catered for. Be it a well-organized Chevras Tehillim or an elaborate Melava Malka, both feature dynamic speakers and delightful menus, aiming to bring a smile to the face of every child.

Mentor Program

Haven of Empathy

They know what it’s like!” they exclaim incredulously. While the shoes can never be entirely filled, women and men who have personally faced the loss of a parent are alwaysready to answer questions, provide support, or simply lend a listening ear to the children in need.

Simcha Packages

Haven of Unity

It’s a joyous occasion – yet tinged with a touch of sadness due to the absence of a parent. Ohr Chodosh rises to the occasion, delivering a beautiful simcha package complete with a thoughtful gift to help fill the void. Moreover, kallahs receive a heartfelt, detailed letter sharing important sentiments upon their special day.

Trips

Haven of Adventure

Ohr Chodosh plans enjoyable excursions for girls to relax and replenish their resilience. In a fun way, they gear up to face the everyday challenges that accompany orphanhood.

Events

Haven of Camaraderie

There’s a reason every member eagerly counts down to an Ohr Chodosh event. These events feature impactful programs of inspiration, where hearts unite, and spirits soar, leaving everyone craving MORE!

Nesiyas

Haven of Discovery

Each year, prior to Rosh Hashana, bachurim embark on a trip to kivrei tzaddikim – a meaningful opportunity to daven. Is there a better way to gather zechusim for the upcoming year?

Yahrtzeit Packages

Haven of Warmth

Before the yahrtzeit, Ohr Chodosh steps in to provide support with a package containing valuable articles, tefillos, candles, and all the pertinent information related to a yahrtzeit.

Playhouse

Haven of Love

An open door now holds a whole new significance. Step inside our fully stocked playhouse, brimming with an array of toys, books, and activities tailored for all ages. It’s a welcoming invitation to children seeking a haven to unwind after a hard day or embrace moments of joy on any day.
